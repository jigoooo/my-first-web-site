LUCY 루시

Jannabi is a group sound band currently active in Korea. Jannabi's vocalist is Choi Jeong-hoon. Jannabi's representative song is 'For Lovers Who Hesitate', 'I Know Where The Rainbow has Fallen'.
'For Lovers Who Hesitate' is the title song included in Jannabi's second full-length album 'Legend'.

They said about the song, "The end is always waiting. It's a song about our complicated mind that knows the logic of love but is deceived as if it will last forever again.
If love comes again, let's fall for it. For those who are hesitant," he introduces. In addition to the title song, 11 songs are included.

In 2020, they won the 17th Korea Popular Music Award for Song of the Year, the 17th Korea Popular Music Award for Best Modern Rock Song, and the 34th Golden Disc Awards for Digital Song.
